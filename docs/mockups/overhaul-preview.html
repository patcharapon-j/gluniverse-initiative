Turn-Advance Animation

Press Advance Turn to see the proposed motion: the previous active card sweeps out with a motion-streak, the rail slides up, the new active card slams in with a light-bloom flare, and a fresh card is dealt in from the bottom. Smoother snap easing and a brighter active impact than current.
